How do you explain biryani in English? What is Biryani? Authentic Indian Food | Little India of Denver Simply put, biryani is a spiced mix of meat and rice, traditionally cooked over an open fire in a leather pot. It is combined in different ways with a variety of components to create a number of highly tasty and unique flavor combinations.
