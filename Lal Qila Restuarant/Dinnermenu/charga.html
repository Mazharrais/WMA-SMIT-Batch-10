Charga Batair

In Pakistan, Bihari Chargha is also known as Tandoori, Roast, or BBQ Bihari Chargha. The simplest recipe on this page is Bihari Chargha. Make sure you get a great deal of praise after making this tasty and tangy Bihari Chargha. This recipe goes well with onion rings, sliced lemon, tomatoes, and cucumbers.
