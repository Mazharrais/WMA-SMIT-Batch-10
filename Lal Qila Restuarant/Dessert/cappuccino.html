Cappuccino Cake

CAPPUCCINO CAKE August 11, 2022 by Ginny Dyer 6 Comments Jump to Recipe Print Recipe cappuccino cake on a black cake stand with espresso beans This is the best cappuccino cake! It has moist coffee cake layers, frosted with coffee buttercream and finished with a coffee ganache drip for the best coffee flavor. This cake is perfect for a special occasion, and all coffee lovers will love it!

Dissolve the instant coffee into the vanilla extract in a ramekin. Then to the butter mixture add the eggs and vanilla/instant coffee mixture and mix on medium speed until pale in color and smooth.
